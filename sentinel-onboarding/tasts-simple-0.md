Ticket A — Environment Intake
Client completes and returns README, client information spreadsheet, environment profile, and Defender XDR guide via secure link.

Step by step guidance provided. End client tenant admin executes delegation directly to our tenant. Platform team validates access to close.

Phase 1 — Assess and Baseline (Days 8 through 15)

Phase 2 — Build and Integrate (Days 16 through 24)
Detection team reviews audit output and deploys applicable rules. Critical asset working session held with client. Watchlists built and silent log detections go live. Data source intake completed collaboratively.

Phase 3 — Validate and Go-Live (Days 25 through 30)
End to end validation and testing completed. Stage to production promotion. Client portal access confirmed. AuditDeck review — the payoff.
